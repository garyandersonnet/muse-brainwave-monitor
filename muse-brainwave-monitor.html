Understanding Brain Waves
What the Muse headband is actually measuring
Delta • 0.5–4 Hz
Deep restoration, sleep, unconscious processing. Strong delta during meditation can indicate profound relaxation or drowsiness.
Theta • 4–8 Hz
Creativity, intuition, light meditation, memory. Many experienced meditators show elevated theta during deep practice.
Alpha • 8–13 Hz
The signature “calm focus” band. Relaxed but alert wakefulness. Muse was designed around training higher, more stable alpha.
Beta • 13–30 Hz
Active thinking, concentration, problem solving — but also anxiety and rumination when excessive. Lower beta = quieter mind.
Gamma • 30–50+ Hz
High-level cognition, insight, “eureka” moments, and cross-brain integration. Rare but very rewarding when it appears during meditation.
